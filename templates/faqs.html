Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book a ticket on TravelGo?

Simply log in, select your mode of transport (bus, train, flight, or hotel), choose the route, fill in passenger details, and confirm your booking.

2. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking under the Dashboard section. For changes, we recommend cancelling and rebooking with the correct details.

3. What payment options are available?

We support UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and popular Wallets.
